Toby Sigle

A strong independant harley chic

Some facts about Toby:

  • She was born in McAllen, TX in 1959.
  • She moved to Wisconsin as a teenager.
  • She was a lesbian, started the Dykes on Bikes Madison WI chapter.
  • She was a Harley Davidson chic rider and belonged to Hog chapters.
  • She was a carpenter and leathersmith.
  • She was involved in many community organizations.
